What is TenAI's Studio?

TenAI's Studio is a hackathon management platform built to organize, run, and evaluate innovation hackathons seamlessly. Whether you're a student competing in your first hackathon, a coordinator managing an event, or a judge evaluating submissions — Studio brings every part of the hackathon experience into one mobile app.

🏆

Hackathon Discovery

Browse, bookmark, and register for upcoming hackathons.

🤝

Team Formation

Create teams, share invite codes, and manage join requests.

🚀

Project Submissions

Submit presentations, demos, and project links with your team.

⚖️

Judging & Evaluation

Criteria-based scoring, feedback, and automated leaderboards.

🔔

Real-time Notifications

Stay updated on team activity, phase changes, and recommendations.

👤

Rich Profiles

Showcase your skills, education, experience, and social links.

About TenAI's

TenAI's Studio is a product of TenAI's Consulting India Pvt. Ltd. — a social enterprise headquartered in Nashik, India. TenAI's was founded on a single mission:

“Nurturing creative talent from rural India to provide excellent services to global clients and build world-class products.”

TenAI's believes that every mind deserves the opportunity to innovate, create, and make an impact — regardless of geography or economic background. The organization collaborates with educational institutions and impact-driven individuals to identify hidden talent, empower them with opportunities, and enable them to deliver exceptional work on a global stage.

What TenAI's Does

  • Studio: Sourcing top talent from rural India through hackathons, innovation camps, and acceleration programs — equipping them with the tools to develop and execute innovative ideas.
  • Consulting: Harnessing rural talent to develop efficient, cost-effective, and innovative solutions for global clients.
  • Products: Building innovative, forward-thinking products for businesses and consumers, crafted by Studio's top talent.

Studio Initiatives

The Studio app is the digital backbone of TenAI's' on-ground programs:

  • Hackathons for Rural Innovators: Hackathons across rural, tier-2, and tier-3 regions to identify creative and technical talent. Students learn Agile methodology, Design Thinking, Elevator Pitching, and Business Model Canvas.
  • Innovation Camps (8th–12th Std): 1.5-day immersive experiences that ignite creativity and entrepreneurial confidence among school and junior college students.
  • Inclusive Camps: Specially structured sessions for differently abled students, ensuring accessibility, confidence building, and meaningful participation.
  • Talent Acceleration & Internship: Selected students progress into project-based internships working on live prototypes in Agentic AI, UI/UX Design, Automation, and Market Research.
  • College Partnerships & Mentorship: Collaborations with educational institutions and retired professors, AI pioneers, and industry experts who mentor the next generation.
